Siapa Kami?

BINTARA CAMAR SECURITY SDN BHD ditubuhkan pada 21 September 2016 sebagai respons terhadap permintaan yang tinggi dalam industri perkhidmatan keselamatan dan sektor-sektor domestik yang lain di Malaysia. Kami berbangga kerana menjadi salah satu syarikat yang mampu menyediakan perkhidmatan keselamatan yang berprestasi tinggi dan berdaya saing.

Our Services

Perundingan Keselamatan Profesional

Kami menawarkan khidmat perundingan keselamatan bagi mengenal pasti dan menangani potensi risiko keselamatan dengan penyelesaian yang efektif.

Pengawal Keselamatan Dalaman Statik

Pengawal kami yang berpengalaman memastikan keselamatan premis dengan rondaan yang konsisten dan pemantauan titik akses.

Pengiring Keselamatan

Kami menyediakan perkhidmatan pengiring keselamatan untuk melindungi individu atau kargo dengan tahap keselamatan yang tinggi.

Our clients

Kementerian Pendidikan Malaysia

We are proud to support Kementerian Pendidikan Malaysia in their mission to provide a safe and secure learning environment for students across the nation. Our dedicated security services are tailored to meet the unique needs of educational institutions, ensuring peace of mind for students, staff, and parents alike.

Pahang Darul Makmur

We are honored to collaborate with the state of Pahang Darul Makmur to provide comprehensive security solutions that protect its facilities and ensure a safe environment for the community. Our commitment to excellence helps uphold safety standards across Pahang, supporting peace of mind for all residents.

Majlis Perbandaran Kemaman

We are proud to collaborate with Majlis Perbandaran Kemaman, providing security solutions that help maintain public safety and enhance the well-being of the local community. Our commitment to excellence supports the municipality’s mission to create a secure and thriving environment for all residents of Kemaman.

Majlis Amanah Rakyat

It is a privilege to work alongside Majlis Amanah Rakyat (MARA) in safeguarding their institutions and supporting their mission to empower communities through education and development. Our specialized security services help provide a secure environment, allowing MARA to focus on nurturing future leaders and innovators.

Pusat Sains Dan Kreativiti Terengganu

We are delighted to support Pusat Sains dan Kreativiti Terengganu in fostering a safe, inspiring environment where creativity and scientific exploration thrive. Our security services are designed to protect both visitors and staff, ensuring that the center can focus on engaging the community with innovative programs and educational experiences.
